Subject: Warden's Nuisance

Entry: 02

Instance: Species

Threat Level: Group

Status: Not Substantial

Summary: The Order of the Watcher is a recently founded cult that worships "The One Who Watches." To the cult, Nero is a divine entity responsible for protecting the cosmos from unforeseen threats that plague the living. To them, the Ward is a sanctuary where their god conducts the necessary procedures to extinguish any harm that may befall their followers.

Although they are not considered a substantial enough threat to warrant containment, they have at times caused disturbances to surrounding government militaries, as they believe the world government is interfering with "Nero's Plan."

On some occasions, the cult has proven to be helpful during containment procedures by providing information and guiding the Ward's personnel toward the threat.

They are known for aiding in the containment of Entry 35, the Cumulus Glass.
